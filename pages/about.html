About MovieVerse

Welcome to MovieVerse! This app allows you to explore a vast database of movies, search for your favorites, view trailers, and save your preferred films using the TMDb API.

Our Mission

At MovieVerse, we strive to provide an easy and enjoyable way for movie enthusiasts to discover new films, access trailers, and keep track of their favorites. Whether you are looking for the latest blockbusters or hidden gems, MovieVerse has you covered.

Features

  • Movie Search: Quickly find movies by title, genre, or release date.
  • Watch Trailers: Get a glimpse of the movie before you watch it with embedded trailers.
  • Save Favorites: Easily save your favorite movies for quick access later.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Enjoy a clean and intuitive design that enhances your browsing experience.

How to Use

  1. Use the search bar to find movies by entering keywords.
  2. Click on a movie card to view details and watch trailers.
  3. Click "Add to Favorites" to save a movie for later viewing.
  4. Access your favorites from the Favorites page.
